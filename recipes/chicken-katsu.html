Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken katsu is a popular Japanese dish consisting of a boneless chicken cutlet (breast or thigh) pounded thin, breaded in airy panko breadcrumbs, and deep-fried until golden and crispy.

Often called a "Japanese schnitzel," it is typically served with a savory, thick tonkatsu sauce, shredded cabbage, and steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasoning (salt and peper)
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Oil

What to serve with the chicken?

  • Tonkatsu sauce
  • Coconut rice
  • Sesame green beans
  • Sunomono
