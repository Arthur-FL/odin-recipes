Shrimp Alfredo

Description

Shrimp Alfredo is a popular American-Italian pasta dish featuring succulent, seared shrimp served over fettuccine noodles, all tossed in a rich, velvety white cream sauce made from butter, Parmesan cheese, and heavy cream.

Often prepared in under 30 minutes, it is a decadent, garlicky comfort food, sometimes enhanced with broccoli or white wine.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces fettuccine or spaghetti
  • ½ pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ small shallot, finely chopped
  • 4 ounces cream cheese
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 ounces grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • ½ tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon lemon zest
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • chopped fresh fresh parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, about 10 minutes. Drain pasta and reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
  2. Meanwhile. season shrimp with lemon pepper and heat oil in a large deep skillet over medium-high heat. And shrimp to skillet and cook for 2 minutes until just browned on one side. Flip and cook for 20 to 30 seconds. Remove shrimp and set aside.
  3. Add shallots to the same skillet and saute for 1 minute. Add cream cheese and milk and cook, stirring constantly until cheese is melted. Add garlic powder and 2/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese. Stir in lemon juice and zest.
  4. Add pasta and shrimp to the cream sauce and stir in as much pasta water as necessary to reach desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve with a sprinkle of fresh parsley.
